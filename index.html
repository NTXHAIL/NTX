Common Questions

FAQ

Everything you need to know about hail repair and working with NTX Hail.

Will my insurance cover the repair?
In most cases, yes. Hail damage is typically covered under comprehensive auto insurance. We have a 100% approval rate and handle all carrier communication directly.
What is paintless dent repair (PDR)?
PDR is a proven technique that removes dents using specialized tools — without sanding, filling, or repainting. It preserves your factory finish and your vehicle's resale value.
How long does a typical repair take?
Most vehicles are completed in 1–3 business days depending on damage severity. We provide real-time status updates throughout the process.
Do you come to me, or do I bring my car in?
Both! We have retail centers in Plano and Denton, TX. For fleet or CAT events, we deploy mobile teams directly to your location anywhere in the country.
Do you work with dealerships?
Absolutely. Our Collision Center Support program and partner portal are designed specifically for dealerships and body shops needing reliable PDR capacity nationwide.
What areas do you serve?
We operate nationwide. From Texas to the East Coast, our teams deploy wherever storms strike — no region is out of reach.
